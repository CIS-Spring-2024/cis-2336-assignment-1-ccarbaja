Meet the Owner:

Carlos Carbajal


Carlos Carbajal is the owner of Sugar Land's Honestly Amazing Cafeteria. What started out as a hobby following alongside YouTube cooking videos quickly turned into a passion. From perfecting the marshmallow bun hotdog to making world renowned chef Gordon Ramsey cry tears of joy from his famous Mtn Dew infused Turkey recipe, Carlos has no shortage of accomplishments in the food industry.


Carlos took over ownership of Sugar Land's Honestly Amazing Cafeteria after a series of riots took place back in 2015. The riots originall started out as a food fight, which eventually turned into fights over food. The original owner decided to punish the members of the Cafeteria by limiting pizza from a weekly basis to a bimonthly basis. This resulted in a mass loss of membership, and the share holders switfly took action by replacing him with Mr. Carlos. Since then, UH Sugar Land's Honestly Amazing has received nothing short of stellar reviews after a massive overhaul of the menu and aesthetic change the cafeteria has underwent.
